This project showcases a high-end Photorealistic Interior Visualization created in Unreal Engine 5. The goal was to achieve a cinematic quality that blurs the line between rendering and reality, focusing on light interaction with interior surfaces and furniture.

Overview

By leveraging real-time technologies, this project explores the potential of immersive architectural visualization, allowing for instant feedback on lighting, materials, and spatial composition without long render times.

Technical Highlights

  • Cinematic Interior Lighting: Utilizing Lumen for real-time global illumination, capturing natural light streaming through windows and its bounce off interior walls.
  • Micro-Detail Materials: Applying high-fidelity PBR textures for fabrics, wood, and metals to ensure every surface looks tactile and realistic.
  • Camera Animation: Smooth, slow-paced cinematic walkthroughs that highlight the spatial flow and interior design details.
  • Dynamic Shadows & Reflections: Precise ray-traced reflections on glass and polished surfaces to enhance the interior's depth.
