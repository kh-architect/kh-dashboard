This project represents a holistic approach to landscape design, transforming a commercial site through three distinct phases: Sketching, Modeling, and Rendering.

Design Phases

  • Phase 1: Sketching: Developed the initial site logic, focusing on circulation paths and defining key entrances to ensure functional flow and accessibility.
  • Phase 2: 3D Modeling: Created a precise architectural model using Autodesk Revit, ensuring all topographical levels, hardscape elements, and building boundaries are technically accurate.
  • Phase 3: Rendering: Produced high-fidelity visuals in Twinmotion to showcase materials, lighting, and the final atmosphere of the outdoor space, capturing the intended user experience.
